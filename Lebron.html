Early Career

1999 - 2003

St. Vincent-St. Mary High School

Lebron launched his career from his high school in Akron, Ohio. His school was named St. Vincent-St. Mary high school. He along with his colleagues brought home 3 state titles. Some of the early awards he obtained were Mr. Ohio Basketball(2001-2003), Mr. Basketball (2002-2003), Gatorade Player of the Year (2002) and became a McDonald’s All American MVP. You can read more about this on the Official Website of Lebron James

The Lebron James Family Foundation

Lebron's Foundation, the LJFF, serves many students and their families! It has a focus on getting the students what they require to succeed, no matter their background. The program targets students from lebrons hometown of akron, ohio. Through its I PROMISE program, the foundation serves more than 1,400 Akron-area students by providing them with the programs, support, and mentors they need to succeed in school and beyond(Lebron James Foundation, N.D.). You can read more about this on the Official Website of the Lebron James Family Foundation, where this excerpt was taken from.
